Welcome to Concentration!

Objective of the game

  • Collect as many matching pairs of cards as possible!
  • The player with the most matching sets of cards, wins.

Instructions

  1. The first player will click two cards, which will then turn from face down to face up to reveal an image.
  2. If the images match, the cards will remain face up and taken out of game play and that player receives one point!
  3. If they do not match, the cards will return to face down mode
  4. REMEMBER where the cards are! Attempt to get a matching pair!
  5. Continue to take turns, clicking on two cards per turn, until all cards are matched and collected by the players.
  6. The player with the most matching pairs, wins!
